# ITC105 – Communication and Information Management

## 📄 Projects Overview

This subject focused on developing communication, research, and information management skills in a professional and academic context.

The main project explored the topic of autonomous driving vehicles, analysing both technical and social implications.

---

## 📊 Project: Autonomous Driving Vehicles (Structured Report)

### 🧩 Objective
Research and analyse the development, impact, and challenges of autonomous driving vehicles, including cybersecurity, ethical, and societal aspects.

---

### 🛠️ What I did
- Conducted academic research using multiple sources (academic and grey literature)
- Structured a formal report (1600+ words)
- Analysed key areas:
  - History and development of autonomous vehicles  
  - Social impact (especially for elderly users)  
  - Cybersecurity risks  
  - Human-vehicle interaction  
  - Ethical and legal challenges  

---

### 🧠 What I learned
- How to structure a formal academic report  
- How to research and evaluate reliable sources  
- Understanding of cybersecurity risks in modern technologies  
- Importance of ethical decision-making in technology  

---

### 📌 Skills developed
- Research and information analysis  
- Technical writing and structured reporting  
- Critical thinking  
- Understanding of emerging technologies  

---

## 📚 Supporting Research Work

As part of the preparation for the final report, I:

## 🎤 Presentation: Ethical Communication (Banksia Case)

### 🧩 Objective
Explain the importance of ethical and non-verbal communication in a global mobility company.

---

### 🛠️ What I did
- Created a structured presentation  
- Analysed communication challenges in multicultural environments  
- Explained key concepts:
  - Ethical communication (transparency, responsibility, confidentiality)  
  - Non-verbal communication (body language, tone, cultural awareness)  

---

### 🧠 What I learned
- Importance of communication in professional environments  
- Role of non-verbal communication in diverse teams  
- How communication affects trust and teamwork
