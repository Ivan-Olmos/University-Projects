## 📈 Project 1: Transformational Leadership Analysis

### 🧩 Objective
Analyse how transformational leadership impacts employee performance and compare it with bureaucratic management theory.

---

### 🛠️ Concepts Covered
- Transformational Leadership
- Bureaucratic Management Theory
- Employee motivation and performance
- Organisational behaviour

---

### ⚙️ What I did
- Analysed an academic article on leadership and performance :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  
- Explained how leadership influences employee motivation  
- Compared two management theories (modern vs traditional)  
- Evaluated strengths, limitations, and real-world applications  
- Developed a structured academic report  

---

### 📊 Key Insights
- Transformational leadership improves motivation and engagement  
- Employee participation increases performance outcomes

- Different management styles suit different environments  
- Importance of balancing structure and flexibility
